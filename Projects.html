Selected projects

A mix of research and applied work. This is a high level overview. I can share papers, reports, or code on request.

Topological phononic ladders

Experimental and numerical study of a long range phononic ladder that supports gapless edge states.

  • Designed lattice parameters and couplings for edge localization.
  • Computed band structures and mode shapes in COMSOL and custom codes.
  • Fabricated and tested with 3D printing and laser vibrometry.

Winding numbers with long range coupling

Analysis of how standard topological invariants break down when interactions extend beyond nearest neighbors.

  • Developed discrete models with multiple coupling ranges.
  • Mapped phase diagrams and compared with numerical spectra.
  • Connected theory to realistic mechanical implementations.

Graphene sheet wave analysis

Study of thick rectangular graphene sheets and small scale effects on dynamics.

  • Used nonlocal elasticity and high order plate theories.
  • Computed natural and bifurcation frequencies under different thicknesses.
  • Explored implications for nanoscale devices and resonators.
Data and complex systems

Applied data projects

Soil parameter prediction

Counter propagation neural networks for estimating soil parameters from limited measurements.

  • Preprocessed geotechnical datasets and engineered features.
  • Trained and validated models on multiple target variables.
  • Evaluated performance and sensitivity for practical recommendations.

Accident severity in Vermont

Machine learning models for predicting road accident severity based on environment and context.

  • Used weather, time, and road information as predictors.
  • Built classification models and analyzed important features.
  • Explored directions for risk mapping and proactive safety.

Malaria prevalence and spatial structure

Spatio temporal modeling of malaria data using R INLA and SPDE based methods.

  • Modeled spatial correlation and time trends in incidence data.
  • Compared different prior and covariance structures.
  • Generated risk maps as a function of location and time.
Text and social data

Language and social signals

Quantifying antisemitism on Twitter

Exploration of annotation schemes and classifier pipelines for detecting antisemitic content in social media.

  • Defined labeling categories with attention to edge cases.
  • Tested baselines and more advanced models for text classification.
  • Discussed limitations and risks of automated moderation tools.
